Key differences in Raynaud's:
- Digital arteries: most affected — blunted or absent flow
- Palmar arch: reduced flow velocity
- Radial/Ulnar: may show increased RI, reduced EDV
- Brachial: often near-normal (proximal vessels less affected)
- Post cold provocation: waveform changes become more pronounced
